Our Story

For many years, Shire-folk minded their own. We tended to our fields, our foals, our families, and, of course, to our food. But the spirit of adventure is pesky and persistent, so we now open our home to you, weary traveler. Come to enjoy the splendid simplicity of life in Hobbiton at Bag End. No foot is too proud to rest here!

Feasts, ales, and the finest leaf in Southfarthing await you!
